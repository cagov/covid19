Questions and answers

What should homeless shelters do to protect clients?

Follow California Business, Consumer Services and Housing Agency guidance for homeless providers and shelters (pdf).

What are the restrictions in my area?

Find out what’s happening in your city by visiting your county or city’s website or calling 311 to get more information about local services in your area. Many counties affected by COVID-19 have published websites about local responses, including:

  • Alameda County
  • Contra Costa County
  • Imperial County
  • Los Angeles County
  • Placer County
  • Riverside County
  • Sacramento County
  • San Bernardino County
  • San Francisco City and County
  • San Mateo County
  • Santa Clara County
  • Santa Cruz County
  • Solano County
  • Yolo County

How is the state protecting imprisoned people in California?

We are dedicated to the safety of everyone who lives in, works in, and visits state prisons. Learn more about these efforts at CDCR Covid-19 Preparedness.
