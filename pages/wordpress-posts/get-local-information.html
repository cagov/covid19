Questions and answers

What are the restrictions in my area?

The state has ordered that certain businesses and activities be restricted in order to slow the spread of COVID-19. See what can be open or must be closed in your county.

Your local health department may have more restrictions. Find your area’s COVID-19 website for more details.

While each county may have different restrictions, it is important to stay home as much as possible. If you do go out, stay 6 feet away from others who are not in your household and wear a mask or face covering. Your actions save lives.
