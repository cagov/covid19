California continues to focus on the safety and well-being of children and students.

On this page:

  • K-12 schools
  • Childcare
  • Camps and supervised youth activities
  • Higher education

K-12 schools

The California Department of Public Health (CDPH) is committed to supporting safe, in-person learning in K-12 schools and limiting the spread of COVID-19.

Find more information:

  • K-12 School Guidance and Resources (CA Safe Schools Hub)

Childcare

The California Department of Social Services (CDSS) and the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) have partnered to provide COVID-19 guidance and resources for Childcare Providers and Parents.

Find more information:

  • Childcare Guidance and Resources (CA Safe Schools Hub)

Camps and supervised youth activities

Supervised programs involving youth can review the K-12 School and Childcare guidance. They should follow relevant strategies to reduce the spread of COVID-19.
