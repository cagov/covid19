Here’s what everyone can do to keep our communities healthy

Do:

  • Wash hands with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.
  • Cover your cough or sneeze with an elbow or tissue.
  • Clean and disinfect frequently touched objects and surfaces.
  • If soap and water aren’t available, use alcohol-based hand sanitizer.

Don’t:

  • Shake hands
  • Touch your face
  • Go near people who are sick
  • Go to the doctor if you aren’t sick
  • Stockpile masks or gloves
