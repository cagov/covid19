Healthcare

  • The state has directed full-service commercial and Medi-Cal plans to waive co-pays, deductibles, or coinsurance for all medically necessary screening and testing for COVID-19.
  • If you don’t have health care coverage or recently lost employer-sponsored health care coverage, see the Health Care Coverage Options Factsheet (pdf) to get covered.
