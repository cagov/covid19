CDPH and a Network of Labs Prepare to Begin Novel Coronavirus Testing in California

The California Department of Public Health announced today that 16 laboratories, including the state’s Viral and Rickettsial Disease Laboratory in Richmond, California, will soon be able to perform testing for the novel coronavirus.
