My Turn COVID-19 Vaccine Scheduling System Now Available in 12 Languages

Today, the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) announced the state's online COVID-19 vaccine scheduling tool My Turn is offering services in a total of 12 languages, allowing more state residents to comfortably navigate MyTurn.ca.gov.
