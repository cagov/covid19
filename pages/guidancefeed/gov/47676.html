February Receipts Beat Forecast by $3.8 Billion, Now Up $14.3 Billion Over January Forecast

SACRAMENTO – Governor Gavin Newsom today announced that General Fund revenues are running $14.3 billion above January’s revenue forecast, with receipts for February exceeding the month’s projections by $3.8 billion.
