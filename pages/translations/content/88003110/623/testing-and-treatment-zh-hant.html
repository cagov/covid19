若您出現冠狀病毒症狀且認為您自己需要接受檢測，請聯絡您的醫療提供者。當地衛生部門和醫療提供者負責逐個排查確定哪些人需要接受檢測，哪些人需要住院治療。

許多新型冠狀病毒患者能夠居家康復。

How to get treatment

Call your doctor for a phone evaluation if:

  • 呼吸困難（呼吸急促）
  • your symptoms (such as fever and cough) are getting worse rapidly
  • 無法居家治療這些病症

如需前往醫院，請提前致電，以便他們為您的到來做好準備。If you call 911, tell the operator you have coronavirus symptoms so ambulance workers can be ready to treat you safely.

How to get tested

加州的冠狀病毒檢測能力日益增強。Governor Newsom announced COVID-19 Testing Task Force, a public-private collaboration that is working to scale up testing quickly.

Who can get tested

檢測能力提升時，當地衛生官員將可視情況擴大檢測規模。Priorities include:

第1級

  • 住院患者
  • Healthcare workers, first responders, and other social service employees
  • Any symptomatic person
  • People identified for testing by public health contact investigations and disease control activities in high-risk settings
  • Asymptomatic residents or employees of congregate living facilities including:
    • 設施內確認有呈陽性病例後
    • Before resident admission or readmission to a facility
  • People in essential jobs

第2級

  • Lower risk asymptomatic people

社區監督

  • Asymptomatic people as part of community or regional surveillance programs

如需瞭解更多有關檢測和治療的資訊，請參閱加州公共衛生署冠狀病毒最新消息。

常見問題與解答 (Project Baseline)

How do I get a drive-through test?

此過程分為兩步：篩查和檢測。首先，您需創建一個帳戶並進行線上篩查。若依據您填寫的篩查表答案確定您現在有資格接受檢測，您將會收到有關如何以及在何處接受檢測的詳細資訊。檢測後，當您的新型冠狀病毒檢測結果出來時，相關部門會透過電子郵件或電話告知您。

為什麼需要創建一個帳戶？

為了使Baseline新型冠狀病毒計劃發揮效用，Verily需要一種能夠驗證使用者身份以適當保護其資訊的方法。由於Google帳戶能夠提供一流的身份驗證服務，因此Verily選擇Google所提供的此項服務。我們要求您連結到現有的Google帳戶或創建一個新的Google帳戶（可以使用任何電子郵寄地址完成此操作）。這樣，我們不僅可以驗證您的帳戶身份，還可以在篩查和檢測過程中安全、私密地與您聯絡。Baseline新型冠狀病毒計劃使用者針對篩查提供的所有資料均單獨存儲，未直接連結到使用者的Google帳戶。

Testing for underserved communities (OptumServe)

The State of California has joined together with OptumServe to expand testing services for underserved communities and some of California’s most vulnerable patients. 

Tests are by appointment only. Make an appointment at https://lhi.care/covidtesting. If you do not have internet access, call:1-888-634-1123.

Anyone who meets the testing criteria can be tested at an OptumServe community testing site, including uninsured, underinsured, undocumented and homeless individuals. OptumServe will generate a unique identification number for individuals who do not have a driver’s license. 

Payment: If you have medical insurance, OptumServe will bill your insurance company. Tests for uninsured individuals will be paid for by the state.
