Coronavirus COVID-19 Response

COVID-19 is a new illness that can affect your lungs and airways. It's caused by a virus called coronavirus.

As of Friday, March 13, there are 198 positive cases and 4 deaths in California. See the California Department of Public Health (CDPH) for the latest data.

Education

All schools, workplaces, and businesses should encourage employees to stay home if sick. See guidance from the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC): Keeping Workplaces, Homes, Schools or Commercial Establishments Safe (pdf)

  • Different measures will be appropriate for each school or district. See the CDPH Guidance for Schools and Guidance for Colleges and Universities.
